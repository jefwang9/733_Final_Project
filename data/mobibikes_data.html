DATA NOTES
  • ​Departure and Return times of trips have been rounded to the nearest hour to maintain the privacy of our users.
  • Accounts have been anonymized to maintain the privacy of our users.
  • Trips made by our Operations team for purposes of rebalancing and maintenance have been removed.
  • A 'Stopover' is when a bike is locked using the cable lock rather than at a station. Please note that a bike's timer continues to run when in Stopover mode.
  • This data may only be used in accordance with our Data License Agreement.
